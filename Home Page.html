Giving you the knowledge to make more informed bets

Why?

We want to help people make more informed bets!

Most people will not win against the house when making bets either in house or online. According to JV Miller, writing for his newsletter, states that professional sports bettors rarely sustain a long-term winning percentage higher than 55 percent, and it's often as low as 53 or 54 percent. This means even the professionals are hardly making money when it comes to sports betting.

Who?

For those who will always bet and want to be better informed!

We want to help those who will bet no matter what was happening the world to give them more information to give them a better chance to gain a winning percentage against the house. There are many sport betters that could benefit from more knowledge about the game they are betting on as well as what goes into the odds that place bets.

How?

Through machine learning!

We queried through our data set to make the best data columns in order to optimize our machine learning process. We took a variety of our data and simplifies it into a 0 or 1 answer in order to make our process of machine learning as simple as possible for example we changed for our over under prediction we made a column where 1 was over the bet and 0 was under the bet. This makes predicting the outcome much easier for our machine learning.

What?

From our project we hope to better inform people about Vegas and its odds
After working through all of what we have presented we hope that many will be better informed to be more confident in making a winning bet. There are many different factors that go into what makes the Vegas odds and opened the hood on some of these with the most important being the total score.

Here is what we found!

Our machine learning program got an accuracy rating over 88% when working with our test data. This means that it accurately predicted 88% of the time when the over or under hit predicting around 56% of the time the over will hit. To understand how our machine learning program was making this determination we decided to look at which feature was most important for our program. What we found was that our 5th feature was the most important which was the total score. This makes a ton of sense as the total score is what is the over/under line is based upon. That however doesn't mean that the other features were completely useless, they still had an impact on our program.
